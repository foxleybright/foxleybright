About

Foxley Bright is a storyteller with a heart full of wonder and a pen dipped in sunshine. Inspired by nature, childhood curiosity, and the quiet magic of everyday moments, Foxley crafts tales that delight, comfort, and spark the imagination. When not writing, Foxley can often be found wandering through forests (real or imagined), always chasing the next story hidden in the rustle of leaves.
